## Detailed Description

### Vishpr Browser Agent - AI-Powered Browser Automation

Control your browser with plain English commands. Vishpr uses advanced AI to understand what you want and executes actions on any website.

**What can Vishpr do?**

- Click buttons and links automatically
- Fill out forms with your data
- Navigate between pages
- Read and extract information from pages
- Scroll and interact with dynamic content
- Execute multi-step workflows

**How it works:**

1. Open Vishpr from the side panel
2. Type what you want to do in plain English
3. Watch as Vishpr executes your commands

**Examples:**

- "Fill out this contact form with my info"
- "Click the submit button"
- "Scroll down and find the pricing section"
- "Navigate to the settings page"
- "Read the main article and summarize it"

**Features:**

- Works on any website
- Natural language understanding
- Multi-step task execution
- Side panel interface for easy access
- DevTools integration for debugging

**Requirements:**

- OpenRouter API key (get one at openrouter.ai)
- Chrome browser

**Privacy First:**

- All data stays on your device
- Your API key is never shared with us
- No tracking or analytics
- Open source
